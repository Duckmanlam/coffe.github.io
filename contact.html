Coffee Story

The exact origins of coffee are shrouded in legend, but it's widely believed to have originated in the highlands of Ethiopia. Centuries ago, a goatherd named Kaldi noticed his goats behaving strangely after eating berries from a certain bush. Curious, he tried the berries himself and experienced a surge of energy. Word of this miraculous plant spread, and soon, people were roasting and brewing the beans to create a stimulating beverage. From these humble beginnings, coffee has evolved into a global phenomenon, shaping cultures and societies around the world.

Our Story

At the crossroads of creativity and tradition, Phan17 Cafe was born. As whispers of old recipes and fresh brews came together, Phan17 emerged, a place where each cup tells a story and each flavor speaks of heritage. Defying the conventional, Phan17 ventured to capture the soul of coffee culture, delivering experiences that are as bold and unique as its guests. From the first sip to the last drop, Phan17 Cafe remains dedicated to creating unforgettable moments, inviting everyone to savor not just coffee, but a sense of connection, warmth, and belonging.
