Our Story

Vietnamese coffee is renowned for its bold, rich flavor and unique brewing traditions. Grown in the lush, fertile highlands of regions like Buon Ma Thuot and Dalat, Vietnam is one of the world's largest coffee producers, specializing in robusta beans. Traditional Vietnamese coffee is often brewed using a phin filter, which creates a slow-dripped, aromatic brew, enjoyed either black or mixed with sweetened condensed milk for a signature creamy and flavorful taste. This distinctive style captures the vibrant and diverse coffee culture that has become a beloved part of daily life in Vietnam.
